To complete the registration process please,
make the following steps:


Contents:

  1. In the letter you have received after buying the game mark the extract of text between the lines
    "------begin key------" and "-------end key-------" with your mouse left button pressed.



  2. Copy the text of the registration key to clipboard - click the right button of your mouse on the marked text and select ' Copy '.



  3. Then go back in this window. Paste the copied text of registration key into registration field that has appeared - click the right button of your mouse in this field and select 'Paste'.



    You will see your registration key appeared in the window.



  4. Press the 'Register' button.



  5. Restart the game.You will see "Owned by: Your Name" in the game menu

    If you'll experience any problems with the registration procedure, please report to support@alawar.com Include your order ID into the body of a report message.
